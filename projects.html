PROJECT GREENPOINT

In a post-apocalyptic world ravaged by monstrous creatures, two teenagers rise as humanity's last hope. Recruited by a secret organization that transforms orphaned children into elite super soldiers, they fight to reclaim the earth from devastation. In this thrilling animated short, we follow their journey of survival, courage, and the battle against overwhelming odds.

FAKUGEZI GAME JAM

The Fak'ugesi Animation Jam was an intense 48-hour creative challenge where we were tasked with producing an animated video from start to finish. With the theme centered around South African hip hop, we drew inspiration from the vibrant culture and music that defines the genre. It was a whirlwind of collaboration, creativity, and innovation, as we pushed our skills to the limit to bring our animated vision to life within the tight deadline.

CORPORATE STYLE ADVERT

The corporate-style video, titled "Introduction to Sustainable Living," is a concise, one-minute explainer animation that combines motion graphics with frame-by-frame animation to deliver its message. This dynamic approach captures viewers' attention, bringing sustainability concepts to life in a way that makes complex ideas accessible and engaging. The video creatively uses animation to highlight practical steps for living more sustainably, guiding viewers through impactful ways to help protect the planet.
