PROJECT GREENPOINT

The project is a 3-to-5-minute animation based on the Animation IV project by group members Bumani Maroleni and Mawethu Nala. The animation will be taken from the initial animation and expanded upon. It is essentially a proof of concept ideal for a portfolio for animation. The animation will be complete with rendered visuals, SFX and voice acting. The project will be done throughout semester 2. It's a great opportunity to showcase our animating skills, create a powerful narrative and produce a high-quality animation piece to showcase in our professional portfolios.
